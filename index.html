About Me

After graduating from the University of California Irvine, with a degree in Economics, I worked as a Tech Risk Consultant for RSM. During my time at RSM, I gained exposure to the development process and the various programming languages each client used. This piqued my interest in web development and it has become a serious passion, in which I actively matured at an accelerated Web Development Program.

My hobbies, including skateboarding, rock-climbing, and hip-hop choreography, reflect my joy in mastering new skills and overcoming difficult obstacles, as well as an appreciation for artistic design. Web-development has become more than a career endeavor, it has become a passion that helps me practice these disciplines on a daily basis.
